Ingredients

  • 300 g tipo 00 flour (W280)
  • 190 g mineral water at 20°C
  • 6 g sea salt
  • 1 g fresh yeast
  • 160 g San Marzano DOP tomatoes
  • 110 g Mozzarella di Bufala Campana DOP
  • 6 basil leaves
  • 12 ml extra virgin olive oil

Method

  1. 1.Autolyse flour and 55% of the water for 30 minutes to hydrate gluten evenly.
  2. 2.Add dissolved yeast, remaining water, and salt, mixing until the dough reaches 26°C core temperature.
  3. 3.Bulk ferment 18 hours at 18°C, then ball to 260 g portions and rest another 6 hours.
  4. 4.Crush tomatoes by hand with 0.5% salt, keeping seeds for natural pectin, and drain for 20 minutes.
  5. 5.Bake at 485°C on a stone floor for 90 seconds, rotating twice to balance leoparding.

Operational FAQ

Can sourdough replace fresh yeast without failing AVPN rules?

Yes, provided the lievito madre is refreshed every 4 hours and the dough pH stays above 4.3; AVPN bulletin 4/2024 confirms parity if fermentation remains under 24 hours.

Why is buffalo mozzarella drained twice?

Bufala Campana DOP carries 58% moisture; double draining lowers surface water so the pie stays under the 68% target water activity that Neapolitan ovens can evaporate in 90 seconds.

Is steel acceptable instead of stone at home?

A 12 mm steel achieves 430°C in standard ovens and returns 15% faster heat than cordierite, but you must limit bake time to 110 seconds to avoid over-dehydrating the cornicione.
